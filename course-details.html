The OTHM Level 5 Diploma in Education and Training's main goal is to equip learners with the fundamental information, comprehension, and skills they need to demonstrate their occupational competence. This includes organizing and carrying out lesson plans and sessions, negotiating individual goals, utilizing a variety of teaching, and learning methodologies, communication problems, and evaluation techniques. All students must have a means of obtaining at least 100 hours of instruction and at least eight hours' worth of practice observations. 

Our Level 5 Diploma in Education and Training at Teaching Academy can assist you in advancing your profession. This is the ideal approach to advance your expertise as a trainee teacher if you have already finished levels 3 and 4. It will assist you in leading learning sessions, organizing lesson plans, and creating teaching and learning methodologies. 

Depending on your prior education and qualifications, you might be able to attain Level 5 before completing Levels 3 or 4. Please get in touch with us for more information about your specific situation. 

Rest assured that we also offer Level 3 Award and Level 4 Certificate in Education and Training online (VLE) courses. 

You can enrol today and start learning immediately!

Career Progression


The learners who successfully complete this degree are eligible to pursue the status of QTLS -Qualified Teacher Learning and Skills. This is granted by SET - the Society for Education and Training, the sector's professional body for persons working as teachers and trainers, though an administered process known as professional formation.

This qualification has been approved and is governed by Ofqual - Office of the Qualifications and Examinations Regulation. Therefore, Learners are qualified to advance to pertinent qualifications at higher levels.
This is the right course for anyone who is currently working in education and wants to earn Qualified Teacher Status (QTLS) or just develop their teaching abilities is encouraged to enrol on this course.

The OTHM Level 5 Diploma in Education and Training (DET) in the UK is designed for individuals who aspire to pursue a career in education and training, particularly in further education (FE), adult education, or vocational training settings. 

This comprehensive qualification is suitable for a diverse range of learners and professionals. This broad and robust program is well-suited for those looking to excel in educational careers and make a positive impact on learners' lives.

Enrolling in the level 5 DET does not need that you have finished the level 3 or level 4 courses. You can enrol on the level 5 course right away if you satisfy the minimal requirements outlined in the FAQs.
For entry onto the OTHM Level 5 Diploma in Education and Training qualification, learners must:

Hold a relevant vocational and/or academic qualification of at least the level above that of their learners and ideally a minimum Level 3 qualification in his/her own area of specialism.
Have access to 100 teaching practice hours during which learners have full responsibility for planning, delivering, and assessing specific area/s of the institution’s provision.
Minimum core of literacy, language, numeracy, and ICT
Learners must be 19 years old or older at the beginning of the course.


English requirements


If a learner is not from a majority English-speaking country, they must provide evidence of English language competency.

Our Level 5 DET program typically covers a wide range of topics to equip educators with the knowledge and skills needed to excel in teaching and training roles. It places a strong emphasis on reflective practice, inclusivity, research, and leadership within an educational context.

Study Hours

These can be divided however you’d like over a 12-month period.

Course Schedule

You are able to divide your hours across a year. It is possible to finish the course in a few months or over the period of a year. The majority of students will finish the program in 9 to 12 months.

Assessment

Observations, classroom teaching, assignments

Course Delivery

The qualification is designed to be delivered over one academic year for full-time study, but it is also flexible in its delivery in order to accommodate part-time and distance learning.

The qualification is delivered face-to-face, through lectures, tutorials, seminars, distance and through flexible online (VLE).

Assessment Training

The Level 4 Certificate in Leading the Internal Quality Assurance of
Assessment Processes and Practice in the UK is designed for individuals
who are or aspire to take on leadership andmanagerial roles in the field
of quality assurance within educational and vocational training settings.

It is an essential qualification for those who aspire to lead and oversee quality
assurance efforts in educational and vocational contexts, contributing to the
overall quality and success of educational programs and training initiatives.

IQA Training

IQA Training

The Level 4 Award in the Internal Quality Assurance of Assessment
Processes and Practice equips learners with the knowledge and skills
needed to effectively carry out internal quality assurance activities. It
is an essential qualification for those who play a critical role in maintaining
assessment quality, ensuring compliance with standards, and contributing
to the overall success of educational and vocational programs.

The Level 4 IQA qualification is more appropriate for students pursuing or
working in an internal quality assurance position. As part of your
qualification, two assessors will need to work with you.

Teaching Assistant Training

This qualification is particularly suitable for Experienced Teaching Assistants,
Aspiring HLTA Candidates and Teaching Assistant Supervisors and valuable for
those seeking to excel in roles that involve assisting teachers and enhancing the
educational experience for students with specific requirements or challenges.
This qualification provides a strong foundation for a rewarding career in the field
of education and support services.

It is an important step for teaching assistants who wish to take on more significant
responsibilities and contribute to the overall success of students in educational
settings.

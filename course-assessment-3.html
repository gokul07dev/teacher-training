Level 3 Award in Understanding the Principles and Practices of Assessment ( AUPPA)

The Level 3 Award in Assessing Vocationally Related Achievement provides a strong foundation for individuals seeking a rewarding career in education, training, and assessment.

For those wanting to assess students on recognized courses, the Level 3 Award in Understanding Principles and Practices of Assessment is regarded as an entry qualification. Before beginning to assess in the Further and Higher Education sectors, most training centres would anticipate if you had this assessor qualification.

No prior education or work experience is necessary to begin the level-3 course. Whether you teach music or coach people, this assessment training will be helpful to you. The course is entirely online (VLE) and is recognized by UK OFQUAL. We'll be here to help you.

Awarding Body



OTHM Qualifications are approved and regulated by the Office of Qualifications and Examinations Regulation (Ofqual).

Career Progression


The Level 3 Award in Assessing Vocationally Related Achievement provides a strong foundation for individuals seeking a rewarding career in education, training, and assessment. It offers the skills and knowledge needed to contribute to the development and success of learners in various vocational fields. Graduates can choose to specialize further or pursue advanced qualifications to expand their career prospects in the education and training sector.

This award opens various career paths and opportunities, including Vocational Assessor, Teaching Assistant, Freelance Assessing, and in Further Education.
This Level 3 course (AUPPA) is designed for those who want to learn about the concepts and methods of assessment without having to work as assessors. Only knowledge qualifies for it. It is intended to be for those who do not currently hold the position of assessor or who have only recently begun their work as an assessor in the further education sector. 

The course will be the beginning point for aspiring assessors, a professional development credential for managers, HR, or quality managers, and for those who are not actively practicing.

It is the right choice for those who want to learn more about assessment or who need to be familiar with assessment practice for their job and for those who desire a quick qualification to help them choose a career.

There are no entry requirements, and the course can be taken at various levels.

Unit 1

Understanding the Principles and Practices of Assessment


Module 1

The Key Concepts and Principles of Assessment
The key concepts of assessment, including validity, reliability, and authenticity.
The principles of assessment, such as fairness, flexibility, and transparency.

Module 2

The Legal and Good Practice Requirements in Assessment
The legal and regulatory requirements related to assessment.
Good practice guidelines for conducting assessments.

Module 3

Types of Assessment Methods and Their Uses
An overview of various assessment methods, including formative and summative assessments, observation, questioning, and self-assessment.
The purposes and contexts in which different assessment methods are used.

Module 4

Planning the Assessment Process
How to plan assessments effectively, including setting clear assessment criteria and objectives.
Developing assessment plans and schedules.

Module 5

Involving Learners and Others in the Assessment Process
Strategies for involving learners in assessment planning and processes.
The importance of feedback and communication in assessment.

Our Level 5 DET program typically covers a wide range of topics to equip educators with the knowledge and skills needed to excel in teaching and training roles. It places a strong emphasis on reflective practice, inclusivity, research, and leadership within an educational context.

Study Hours

These can be divided however you’d like over a 12-month period.

Course Schedule

You are able to divide your hours across a year. It is possible to finish the course in a few months or over the period of a year. The majority of students will finish the program in 9 to 12 months.

Assessment

Observations, classroom teaching, assignments

Course Delivery

The qualification is designed to be delivered over one academic year for full-time study, but it is also flexible in its delivery in order to accommodate part-time and distance learning.

The qualification is delivered face-to-face, through lectures, tutorials, seminars, distance and through flexible online (VLE).

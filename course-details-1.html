Level 3 Award in Education and Training (AET) Course

The Level 3 Award in Education and Training (AET) is an introductory qualification for individuals who want to start a career in teaching or training.

The Level 3 Award in Education and Training (AET) is an introductory qualification for individuals who want to start a career in teaching or training. This course equips participants with the essential knowledge and skills required to become effective educators in various settings.

The Level 3 Teaching Qualification we provide at Teaching Academy is the perfect foundation course for anyone seeking to teach in an adult education and skills sector. Regardless of your skill level, you may enrol in this course to get started on the path to your career objective and learn at your own pace because it is entirely available in online (VLE) based learning.

This course takes about 120 hours to complete and requires about 48 hours of study time, plus time for research and assessments. You are free to divide the time as you like. The course can be finished quickly in a few weeks, or gradually over the course of a year.

With this certification, you can excel to the next level of learning, such as , Level 4 Certificate and the Level 5 Certificate.

You can enrol today and start learning immediately!

Awarding Body



OTHM Qualifications are approved and regulated by the Office of Qualifications and Examinations Regulation (Ofqual).

Career Progression


Progression onto the Level 4 or 5 Education and Training courses.
This course is an excellent steppingstone to more specialized credentials that let you instruct and coach. You can learn the fundamentals of teaching and training roles in the adult education and skills sector with the aid of this certificate. The Level 3 AET is the right choice for beginners with little to no prior experience in teaching and training.

The Level 3 AET is a foundational qualification and serves as a prerequisite for higher-level teaching qualifications in the UK, such as the Level 4 Certificate in Education and Training (CET) or the Level 5 Diploma in Education and Training (DET). It's a versatile qualification that caters to a diverse audience looking to enter or advance in the field of education and training.

For entry onto the OTHM Level 3 Award in Education and Training (AET) Course qualification, learners must:

Given the nature of the course, the minimum entry age is 19.
There are no other formal entry requirements for this course.
However, it is recommended that you have an intermediate ability to read and write English.


Unit 1

In this until, you will be understanding the Roles, Responsibilities and Relationships in Education and Training.


This introductory unit aids in your understanding of the fundamentals of teaching, including health and safety, equal opportunity, and potential coworkers.

Unit 2

This unit will help you in understanding and utilizing inclusive learning and teaching strategies in education and training.


The knowledge you gain from this unit will help you better understand how individuals learn, how they communicate, and how to employ a variety of resources and instructional strategies to best meet the requirements of each of your students.

Unit 3

You will be essentially taught about understanding the principles and practices of assessment.


This unit defines assessment and explains how to carry out superior assessment practice and to know if module is perfect for anyone interested in evaluating workplaces.

Study Hours

Approx. 120 hours.

Course Schedule

Your hours can be divided over a 12-month period. The course can be completed in a matter of weeks or over the course of a year.

Assessment

A number of assignments and a micro-teaching assessment.

Course Delivery

A couple of assignments and a micro-teaching assessment.

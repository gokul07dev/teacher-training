The Level 4 Certificate in Education and Training (CET) is an advanced qualification for individuals who wish to enhance their teaching and training skills. This program focuses on developing a deeper understanding of educational principles, instructional design, and effective classroom management.

This course will assist you with analysing, developing, and improving your current skills if you have recently begun or are about to begin teaching.

The Level 4 Certificate in Education and Training online (VLE) course offered by Teaching Academy is flexible, includes top-notch course material, and comes with a tutor to help you. You can enrol in the level 4 course today and begin learning right away because it is instant enrolment.

The Level 3 Award and the Level 5 Diploma in Education and Training are also available online (VLE) at our academy.

Please visit our course page for more details. 

Awarding Body



OTHM Qualifications are approved and regulated by the Office of Qualifications and Examinations Regulation (Ofqual).

Career Progression


After finishing the Level 4 Certificate in Education and Training course or training, you will be in the best possible position to improve yourself and strengthen your career-related abilities in the relevant Education and Training industry.
The Level 4 Certificate in Education and Training (CET) in the UK is designed for individuals who aspire to become educators or trainers in a variety of educational and training settings. This qualification is suitable for a range of learners and professionals, including Aspiring Teachers, Trainers and Assessors, Career Changers, Learners, and Support Staffs

It's important to note that while the Level 4 CET is a valuable qualification for educators and trainers, it may also serve as a steppingstone for those interested in pursuing higher-level teaching qualifications, such as the Level 5 Diploma in Education and Training (DET) or a relevant degree in education.

Ultimately, the Level 4 CET is a versatile qualification that caters to a broad audience, offering opportunities for skill development, career advancement, and professional growth in the field of education and training.
